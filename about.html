About Us

Our Mission

Welcome to "vastawaala"! We are a company that is dedicated to providing high-quality clothing and home decor products to our customers. Our mission is to provide our customers with the best possible shopping experience by offering a wide range of products that are made with the highest quality materials and designed to meet our customers' needs and preferences.

We are committed to supporting "Make in India" and "Khadi India" initiatives by sourcing our products from local artisans and manufacturers. By doing so, we aim to promote sustainable and ethical fashion while supporting local businesses and communities.

We offer a unique "Rent Now, Wear Now, Pay Later" option for our customers, allowing them to rent clothing for special occasions or everyday wear and pay for it later. This option makes it easy for our customers to stay on-trend without breaking the bank.

We have a special inclination towards ethnic wear and offer a wide range of products to cater to our customers' ethnic wear needs. We also offer a customization option, allowing our customers to design and customize their own dresses to suit their individual preferences.
